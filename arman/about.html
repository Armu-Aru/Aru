About Me

Hey! I'm Arman Singh Jaryal, currently an MSc student at the National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar. I am interested in bioacoustics, biodiversity monitoring, machine learning, and acoustic signal processing.

My current thesis research focuses on understanding species richness in Ensiferan communities through passive acoustic monitoring, and machine learning algorithms.

Outside academics, I enjoy climbing, badminton, reading philosophy, and engaging in nature.
